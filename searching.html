Searching for a New Position

Subjecting yourself to a new job search can be grueling and having the knowledge of what is available that is the “right fit”, based upon your experience, can be daunting. Trying to submit resumes, schedule interviews, research a prospective new employer, and preparing yourself for the process can exhaust you just thinking about it.

Marathon Search provides every candidate with a service that will schedule the interview, help prepare you for meeting with the hiring authorities, and give you all of the necessary lowdown on what the job which you are applying for entails. We are able to answer all of the questions that you may feel uncomfortable asking in the interview process. Marathon Search recruiters have the experience to help you determine if the position that you are interested in is the right one for you.

We will discuss salary expectations, how to market your skill set, how to approach the interviewing process, how to develop realistic expectations, and strategies for your best representation. We know what it takes to land those best positions with the best companies, providing that you have the required qualifications. A stable track record is a must.
