For Hiring Managers

Finding the top talent in the hospitality industry is every hiring authority’s ultimate challenge. If you are scouring through the databases of Monster, Hotjobs, Careerbuilders, HCareers, and the like- you are only seeing those desperate souls who are out there searching and are visible to everyone- and most likely their current employers. These are the candidates who are either unemployed or don’t care about confidentiality.

In either case, they are not the top tier candidates in the restaurant industry. The best talent is very selective and surreptitious about their job searches. They will systematically observe and select their next employer. They will not jump at the first opportunity to present itself. They will research and explore the company before they make their move.

We at Marathon Search excel at finding this calibre of candidate. If you want to hire the best talent for your staffing needs, and assuming that your company is performing well in its market segment, we are able to put the top results getters in front of you- at any level.
